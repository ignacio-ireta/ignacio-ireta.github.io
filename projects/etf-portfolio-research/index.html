Research system

ETF Portfolio Research

A reproducible research pipeline for ETF portfolio construction, optimization, walk-forward backtesting, reporting, and tracked assumptions. The goal is not to produce magical answers; it is to make portfolio decisions inspectable.

System assay

candidate allocations

Constrained optimization Universe definition, configurable objectives, and explicit constraint handling.
Temporal validation Trailing-data-only evaluation to avoid lookahead bias.
Generated artifacts HTML, Excel, figures, and JSON metrics generated from code-driven outputs.
Assumptions made visible Modeling choices stay documented instead of disappearing into intuition.

Why it matters

Portfolio discussions often hide assumptions behind confidence. This project tries to do the opposite: expose the assumptions, version the choices, and make results easier to challenge.
