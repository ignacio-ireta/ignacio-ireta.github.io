Working notes

A practice built around careful prototypes.

Ignacio works across AI, cognition, memory, finance, health, languages, and biological systems. The common thread is a preference for patient inquiry: make the question visible, test the assumptions, then shape the result into something another person can use.

The projects here sit between engineering, experimentation, and interpretation, where careful thinking matters as much as implementation.
